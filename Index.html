Service Provider Processes: Automation vs. Orchestration

Date: November 2023

Understanding Service Provider Processes

In the modern service provider landscape, three core processes are essential:

  • Order Process: Handles customer requests, service validation, and initial setup
  • Build Process: Manages service implementation, resource allocation, and configuration
  • Run Process: Ensures ongoing service delivery, monitoring, and maintenance

The Role of Automation

Automation is crucial for:

  • Reducing manual errors
  • Speeding up routine tasks
  • Ensuring consistency in service delivery
  • Improving operational efficiency

The Need for Orchestration

Orchestration goes beyond automation by:

  • Coordinating multiple automated processes
  • Managing complex workflows across systems
  • Ensuring proper sequence and timing of operations
  • Providing end-to-end service management

Conclusion

Both automation and orchestration are essential for modern service providers. While automation handles individual tasks efficiently, orchestration ensures these automated processes work together seamlessly, creating a comprehensive service delivery system.
